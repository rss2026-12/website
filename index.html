Meet the Team

Yaqi Li

yaqili@mit.edu

Yaqi Li is a sophomore majoring in Computation and Cognition with a minor in Design. Her work explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, design, and human-robot interaction. She has conducted research with the MIT Media Lab's Critical Matter Group on 3D-printed textiles and human-AI interaction, and is currently studying multimodal machine learning with tactile sensing gloves at the Multisensory Intelligence Group. Yaqi is also on the student council of Morningside Academy of Design, an associate advisor for DesignPlus, plays piano with MIT’s Chamber Music Society, and is an active member of the Golf Club.
